Send automated email of budget alert

Idea

  • By default, only the root user receives an email at the beginning of a month about the payment of the cost used the last month.
  • To detect the unexpected cost jump as soon as possible, set a budget and send an alert, for example, to an email address.

Steps

  • Sign in to AWS console by a user with access to billing
  • Go to AWS Budgets
  • Click Create a budget
  • Choose Cost budget, Click Set your budget
  • Name
    • Monthly AWS budget
  • Period
    • Monthly
  • Budget effective dates
    • Recurring budget
    • Start month, this month
  • Budgeted amount
    • For example, $100
  • Click Configure thresholds
  • Thresholds
    • For example, threshold 1, actual cost: 50% of budget amount, email recipients: your email
    • Threshold 2, actual cost 100% of budget amount, email recipients: your email
  • Click Confirm budget
  • Click Create

Test

  • Test whether we can actually receive an alert.
  • Go to AWS Budgets
  • Click the budget name we just made
  • Click Edit Budget
  • Click Configure thresholds
  • New threshold
    • Actual cost, set a small dollar amount for absolute value which is above your spending in the current month to Alert threshold.
  • Email recipients
    • Enter the same email address.
  • Click Confirm budget
  • Click Done
  • The email will not come quickly even if the AWS already exceeds the threshold. Probably several hours later.
  • When you receive an AWS Budgets email, we confirm that AWS budget alert is working.
